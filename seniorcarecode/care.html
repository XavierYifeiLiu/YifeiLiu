Senior-Care Support

Care Giver Services
You are a caregiver if you help an older or dependent adult with any of the following:-
  • Preparing Meals
  • Bathing and Getting Dressed
  • Grocery Shopping
  • Cleaning the House
  • Managing Medications
  • Arranging for Services
  • Paying Household Bills
  • Getting In and Out of Bed
  • Getting to Doctor Visits
